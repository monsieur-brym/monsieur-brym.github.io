THE MAN BEHIND THE DM SCREEN

I am a 21 year old Data Analytics undergrad at Arizona State University. I have always loved fiction, fantasy, and anthropology. For me, Dungeons and Dragons has been a way for me to talk and think about what I love, while getting closer with my little brothers and friends.

This is a common story for people who play D&D. It starts as something silly, but grows to become so much more!

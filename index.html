ROLL FOR INITIATIVE, ADVENTURER

Will you fight for good, or evil? For honor, or for greed? The choice falls in your hands when you are at the table. That is, if the dice are on your side.

When beginning to play the best tabletop game ever, it can be difficult to know where to start. Please enjoy the resourses on this website as a map for your journey. If you need a place to start, navigate to the "How To" section. If you are interested in the history of D&D, what it is, or my experiences with the game; we have all of that as well!
